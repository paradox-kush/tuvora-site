Delete your Tuvora account

This page applies to accounts in the Tuvora and Tuvora TV apps (developer: Tuvora / paradox-kush).

Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Tuvora on your phone and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Or request deletion by email

Email rockkevin47@gmail.com from the address you registered with, with the subject "Delete my Tuvora account". We process requests within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email and credentials).
  • All synced data: profiles, watch progress and history, library, collections, preferences, followed leagues, and synced playlist configurations including their credentials.

Nothing is retained after deletion. Anonymous crash/usage analytics are not linked to your account and cannot be traced back to you.

See also the privacy policy.
